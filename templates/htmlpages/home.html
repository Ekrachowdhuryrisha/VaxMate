About Us

VaxMate is a student-developed web platform designed to empower families across Bangladesh with comprehensive vaccination management tools. Born from the need for accessible, reliable healthcare solutions, we created a user-friendly system that bridges the gap between families and essential vaccination services. Our platform offers more than just reminders—it's a complete vaccination ecosystem. From intelligent scheduling that accounts for age-appropriate vaccines to real-time updates on government immunization programs, VaxMate ensures you never miss a critical dose. Our family tracking feature allows parents to monitor vaccination records for children, elderly relatives, and extended family members all in one secure dashboard. Built with the local context in mind, VaxMate integrates with Bangladesh's national vaccination calendar and provides location-specific information about nearby health centers, mobile vaccination units, and special immunization campaigns. We partner with local health authorities to deliver accurate, timely information that families can trust. Our mission is simple: make vaccination management as easy as checking your email. By combining modern technology with healthcare expertise, we help families stay protected, reduce missed appointments, and contribute to building a healthier Bangladesh. Join thousands of families who trust VaxMate to keep their loved ones safe and healthy.
